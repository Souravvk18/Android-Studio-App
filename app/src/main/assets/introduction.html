10 ideas to Earn Money online and generate passive income.

 

Passive income is the one which generates constant money with little investment.

When a source of income is passive income, that means it requires no or little maintenance to keep the cash flowing.

Obviously, generating passive income is difficult. It requires an initial investment of a significant amount of time, and we generally do not see short-term income.

 Months and years can even go by without us seeing a little amount of money from these activities.


The truth in this matter is that time is much more valuable than money, because although money can be spent and then earned back, time is only spent, and then it is gone forever.

As we grow up in life, we understand the importance of time and being able to choose what we want to do and what should we do with those precious moments that we all have in life.
