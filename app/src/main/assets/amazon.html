What is Amazon Affiliates?


AMAZON AFFILIATES is Amazon's affiliate program, through which you can sell the products from its extensive catalog online.


 As an intermediary and take a commission of up to 10% (depending on the product) for each sale you make.


This is also known as passive income since you make the sale, but Amazon takes care of everything else and you don't have to worry about a thing.


For this, you will have a personalized affiliate ID, which will help you generate your special links and will allow Amazon to identify that this sale corresponds to you.


What do you need to start making money with Amazon?


To start working as an Amazon affiliate and earn income, you basically have to have a website or mobile app, create an account, and generate traffic.


Joining the program is free, but before you can start using affiliate links, you will need to have Amazon approve your account, which usually happens within 12-24 hours if everything is in order.


1)- write quality texts and have enough content


Although this may vary depending on the case, it is recommended that, before making your request to Amazon, your website is published with a minimum of 15-20 articles.


Amazon will appreciate that these articles are original and well written, oriented to the interest of the users, without spelling mistakes or grammatical errors, etc.


2)- Correctly include your ID in the affiliate links


It may seem like a no-brainer, but being careless with this is something that happens more than you might think.

 In these cases, Amazon ignores everything.


Amazon will only pay you the commissions generated from links that are properly formatted.


To do this, always make sure that you are logged in with your affiliate account when searching for products on Amazon to include the links on your website.

 

You can also get a short link, but the full link will allow you to check that your ID is correctly included, preceded by the word “tag”.


In this way, when someone clicks on your link and lands on Amazon to make the purchase, they will access with a URL that includes your affiliate ID.


3.- Do not use the Amazon brand

 

Not at the time of making your request or ever, since as soon as Amazon detects it, it will close your account and you will immediately stop participating in the affiliate program.

 

This implies, for example, that:


  • You cannot include any of the Amazon trademarks in your domain name. This also includes variations of such marks and names that are spelled the same but include some modifications.


  • You cannot use any of the trademarks or variations such as username, group name or any other identification on a social network.


  • You may not take any action that could imply that you are acting  on behalf of or in collaboration with Amazon  (for example, a press release).


  • You cannot use the terms of any of their brands as keywords for your search engine ad campaigns. You can run paid campaigns to drive traffic to your site, but without using Amazon brands.

4)- Do not buy with your own affiliate links


Amazon does not allow you to use your own affiliate links to buy products yourself and earn the corresponding commission.


Yes, it is tempting, because this way it is as if you were buying at a discount, but it is a prohibited practice and therefore a reason for Amazon to close your account immediately.

 

5.- Comply with the content policy


Among the content that Amazon considers "inappropriate" are those that:


  • Promote or contain sexually explicit materials (pornography).


  • Encourage violence or contain violent materials.


  • Encourage or contain defamatory or libelous materials.


  • Promote discrimination (sex, race, religion, nationality, sexual orientation).


  • Promote or carry out illegal activities.


  • Violate intellectual property rights.

 

 

6.- Privacy Policy, Legal Notice, and Cookies


Your website must include a Privacy Policy page or, where appropriate, a Legal Notice page, where you explain to your users the way in which you collect, use, and disclose their data.
