The problem with free Blogs

 

As a blogger, you have the option of creating a blog for free through a variety of websites. 

This sounds like a great idea in theory, but there are some huge downsides to using a free blogging site:-

 

1)- free is not free.


Sites that offer free blogs exist to make money for the company that offers you the “free” blog. They don't give you a blogging space because they like you.

 Instead, they make money from the time and hard work you put into blogging. This is done in a couple of ways:


They sell your ad space.


Free blogging sites will put ads on your blog. You have absolutely no control over what is being advertised on your blog and you will not see a single penny of the money they receive from the sale of this advertising space.


They sell you the "enhancements"


Signing up for one of these services may be free, but you will quickly realize that to make any significant changes to your blog you will have to pay.


 Do you want to change the design of your blog? $ 50. Do you want your own domain name? $ 20 per year.

 

2)- Non-profit:-


The goal of free blogging sites is to make money for the company offering the blog space, not for you. This means that you are not allowed to sell products or services through your blog .


In addition, the company that offers you the blog gets all the profit from the sale of an advertising space on your blog.

 As you'll see in the step-by-step tutorial, selling ad space is a major source of income for most blogs, and it's just not possible with a free blog.


3)- No control


When you start your blog on a free website, you are basically renting your blog space. Because of this, the owner is the one with all the power.

 If they decide to leave you out, for whatever reason, then there is absolutely nothing you can do about it , as you are not actually the blog owner.


To make matters worse, free blogging sites don't give you access to the full range of features you need to become a successful blogger. 

In the step-by-step guide on this site I show you some great tips and tricks that you can use to optimize your blog. Unfortunately, many of these things simply cannot be done on a free blog site.

 

4)- poor security:-


The reality of the Internet is that sometimes sites are hacked. Yes, this happens even to new, smaller blogs.

 If this happens to you, and you are using a free blog provider, the hacker can steal your domain name, which means you will lose your entire site and can never get it back .
