How to do Off-page SEO

 

Once you complete your article with On-Page SEO and your article is as per our On-Page Seo Checklist, then now move on Off-Page SEO.


Every person has their own tricks and strategy for off-page SEO. But here I give you some common and helpful tips for Off-Page SEO.

 

The 2020 Off-Site SEO Checklist.

Here I give you a full checklist about Off-Page SEO.

 

1. Social Sharing


Social Sharing is one of the biggest factors for ranking. If your post or article viral in Social Media then Google give you first priority for ranking.


 So, According to me, when you publish any post at that time you can share this post on Social Media. (Ex-Facebook, Instagram, Linkedin, Etc.)

 

2. Forum Submission


Forum Link is also good for Google Ranking. So, try to go on your relevant niche forms and making links.


 In 2020, Google count only your relevant forum niche link.(Ex - Quora, BlackHatWorld, WordPress, Etc.)

 

3. Profile Backlink


Some time profile backlink is also helpful for ranking. If you work on a Low Competition keyword then you can easily rank using Profile Backlink.

 

4. Guest Post


Guest Post is one of the best methods for building Link. You can write a post for another blog and this blog gives you a backlink.

 Guest Posting is a Safe and Most Powerful Link Building Strategy.

 

5. Make Videos


You can make one video about whatever you can write in your blog. Some users are don't like text content.


 So, you can make 1 video and give your article link in the video description.


 10-20% of Users are the open links from YouTube and other video platforms. All users are 100%, interested Users.
