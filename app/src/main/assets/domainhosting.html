Choose Domain and Hosting

 

Before you can start blogging, there are two things you need to do:


1)- choose a domain-


The domain name for this blog is createunblog.com. If you type that domain name into an Internet browser anywhere in the world, you will be sent to this site.


Uniqueness-


You should choose a name that is unique and descriptive so that your audience will immediately know what your blog is about.


Another option is to simply use your name. For example, I have registered scottchow.com. You can also add your middle name or second initial if you find that your name is already in use.


Memorable:-


Make your domain name memorable. Keep the domain name short. Domain Checker will help you to find the one that fits you best!

Many of the best bloggers use their own name as their domain name. This has the added benefit of preventing someone else from registering your domain, allowing you to secure your "virtual real estate."

Once you have a few name candidates, you will have to choose a domain extension.


Choose TLD(top level domain)


.com is the gold standard, but .net and .org work just as well. I would suggest staying away from other extensions like .us or .biz as they are not that familiar to most people.


It is possible that the domain name you want is already in use. This happens to all of us.


 If you find that the name you want is already in use you can try adding simple words like "the", "a", or "my" to the beginning of the name, or you can try adding hyphens between words like create-a-blog.com.

 

2. Choose a web hosting.
