How does Clickbank work?

 

At Clickbank, you can earn money from home in two ways: as a merchant and as an affiliate.


As a merchant, it is the most complicated method, since you will need to have your own product and that is very, VERY good for it to be sold.


That's why in this article I'm just going to show you how Clickbank works as an affiliate.


The operation is this:


  1. You sign up for Clickbank.
  2. You choose a product (or several) of other people that you want to promote.
  3. When choosing that product, they will give you a link with your affiliate code.
  4. Every time you recommend that product and someone buys it with your link, you will earn a commission of up to 75% of the total price of that product.


You decide to promote a course in garden decoration.


This course costs $ 25. And the commission is 60%.

If you recommend the course and 5 people buy it, the total would be $ 125.


You would receive 60% of that $ 125, which would be $ 75. [/ su_service]
