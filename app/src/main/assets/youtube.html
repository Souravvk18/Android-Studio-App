2. Advertising on Youtube


Therefore, if you want to make a YouTube channel and earn money.


 the first option is to take advantage of the income you will receive from advertising.

which will vary depending on the number of times your viewers see ads on your content. 

 

The money you earn on YouTube for showing advertising depends on:


The type of ad (banner, discovery, skippable or not, etc). 


However, you cannot decide what type of ad will show on your channel, nor can it's content. 


Competitiveness Ads on YouTube work by bid, so you'll earn more money with larger advertisers or those competing for a more saturated niche. 

 

It is important to mention that as a content creator you have no control over the ads beyond choosing when they can be shown.

Tips and advice: How to make videos on YouTube and earn money


To earn money with YouTube it is necessary that your videos receive many visits. 


Here are some tips for your content to become one of the most viewed videos on YouTube : 


Create attractive and informative content: 

Users are always looking for videos that entertain them or give them valuable information.


 If you can provide your audience with quality video content, they are likely to watch the entire video and come back in the future.

 which is vital to making money on YouTube by uploading videos. 


Post regularly and consistently: 

the great advice we give to anyone wondering how to make a YouTube channel and earn money is: be consistent.


 Not only will the YouTube algorithm benefit you, but it's good for creating a stronger relationship with your audience.


 When you maintain a constant stream of content for your viewers, they will continue to interact with your channel.

 This means that they will be much more aware of your online presence and therefore your e-commerce business.


 It is also very important that you pay attention to the best times to publish your content to take advantage of the hours.

 when people are most likely to see your videos, which will help new users discover your channel.


Collaborate with other YouTubers:

 One strategy you can use to earn money from YouTube videos is to collaborate with other YouTubers.


 This is an excellent method that will allow you to increase the presence of your channel, offer unique content, and get your content to a larger audience.


 Do some research on the YouTubers that are active in your target market and get in touch with them;


 they are very likely interested in collaborating. It's very similar to influencer marketing on Instagram, only on YouTube. 


Try to make your videos longer than 10 minutes: 

YouTube videos that are longer than 10 minutes have a longer playback time.


 which contributes positively to the amount of ad revenue that those videos will generate.


 Additionally, videos that have a higher average watch time will appear higher in search results,

 which means that there is a higher likelihood that users will see your content.


 Ultimately, posting longer videos will make you earn more money with YouTube. 


Try to create attractive video titles and thumbnails that appeal to the click.

It is important to keep this in mind when creating content for your channel and above all, when promoting it.


 So remember, YouTube is a very visual-oriented platform, so making attractive designs will help you attract more users

 and, as a consequence, earn more money on YouTube.

 Needless to say, this also plays an important role in your success. 

You need to present your brand in a positive and professional way, so your video content should be of high quality.
