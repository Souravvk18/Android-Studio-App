Promote Your Blog

 

Promoting the blog is an area in which I see that most bloggers fail. There are a ton of great bloggers out there that no one has ever heard of, simply because they don't know how, or just don't want to, promote their blog.


The most important advice I can give you about promoting your blog is don't be shy about telling people about your blog! most effective promotional methods are free!

 

1. Social networks


Most likely, you already have a Facebook account, and maybe even a Twitter account as well. So when you post something on your blog, make sure you also post a link to your blog on your social accounts.


It also helps to connect with other people interested in the topic. Send a friend request to the best bloggers in your niche and be sure to follow them on Twitter too.

 

2. Comment on other blogs


On many blogs you can leave a comment, along with a link to your own blog. If you make helpful comments on related blogs, you will be exposing your blog right in front of the people you know are interested in the topic.


Caution! Do not abuse this method.

If you make a helpful comment, people are more likely to think about visiting your site, as they will want to know more about what you have to say on the subject.

 

3. Link to other blogs


One of the best blogging ideas is to write a reply to a post from another blog. This could be a blog post that you disagree with, or simply a topic that you have something else to add to.


When doing this, make sure you link to the blog you are replying to. In most blogs this creates what is known as a "backlink."


This means that anyone who reads the other person's blog will also see the link to your blog and hopefully click on it to visit your site.


4. Guest post


Another way to reach readers of other blogs to hear about your blog is to write guest posts.

To write a guest post, just contact other blog owners and offer to write a post for their site.


Not all bloggers are open to this idea, but if you can find a few who are, it's a great deal for everyone. You provide them with quality content and in return, you link to your blog in your post.

 If people enjoy your guest post then they are likely to visit your blog to see more great content.

 

5. Online forums


If you haven't already, you should join popular online forums related to your topic. For example, if your blog is about dog training, join a couple of the most popular dog training forums.


Many forums will allow you to post a link to your site in the signature at the bottom of every post you write. Some forums may require you to make a couple of posts before letting you do this.
