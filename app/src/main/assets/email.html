What is email marketing?

Email marketing is a part of the global digital marketing strategy of a company.


 It consists of using email as a means to promote products or services and to develop a relationship with current and potential clients, to help us achieve our marketing objectives.

Why is the email marketing strategy important?


The implementation of an email marketing strategy plan is an essential aspect of the growth and management of your business.


Working on marketing strategies through emails allows you to inform, persuade and win loyalty from your database that may be interested in your product or service.


It is important that you consider a specific call to action aimed at generating sales or traffic to relevant sections of your website.


It is also relevant to consider the creation of community within the strategy, building sustainable relationships through mailings that generate value.


Not having an email marketing strategy means missing sales opportunities and opportunities to strengthen ties with customers.


Start email marketing


To do email marketing you have to keep these objectives in mind:


- Conversion: sale of products or services.

- Customer loyalty

- Brand awareness


Email is a good channel to build a relationship with users. For many customers, the inbox has become a curated space to receive messages from brands that matter to them.


If your brand is communicated directly in the email inbox of a person that is in your database, you have the unique opportunity to be present in their daily life.


This generates more impact than a publication on social networks since working with email marketing ensures that your message reaches the right person.

 

How to do good email marketing?


To do good email marketing you have to consider that it is an opportunity to establish lasting relationships with your clients and potential clients.


The first steps you have to consider:

  • Create your own list: send your emails only to people interested in receiving some kind of information.

      
  • Follow the law: you have to comply with the anti-spam rules.
      
  • A mix of messages: not only send promotional emails but also to establish a good relationship with your customers.

      
  • Respect your subscribers: try to make your customers who have trusted their information feel special.

      
  • Follow a schedule: sending an email on a certain day each week will help your subscribers know what content to expect.

      
  • Optimize for smartphone: if your email is not displayed optimally on mobile devices, your customers are likely to think that you do not know how to prepare an email correctly.

 

Email marketing software


The first step in implementing an email marketing strategy is choosing software for the mailing.


Email marketing platforms have a necessary infrastructure to guarantee mail delivery, this is something that an Internet service provider such as Gmail or Outlook cannot guarantee.


Another important advantage of a mailings service is being able to manage your subscribers and also segment them into lists by different interests or types of campaigns.

 

And if you want a custom email design, this software gives you ready-made templates and easy-to-use email editors.


These platforms also provide information on the performance of the mailing campaign, open rates, click rates, among other indicators.
