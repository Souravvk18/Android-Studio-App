 do you want to make money with Facebook? The first thing you need to know is if the platform allows it.

Requirements to monetize on Facebook

 

Before knowing the most effective ways to make money with Facebook, it is essential that you know what are the requirements that you must take into account.


 These are the conditions stipulated by this social network to anyone who wants to monetize their efforts on the platform:

Facebook Page: 

Although it is obvious, it does not hurt to clarify that you should not try to monetize your efforts on Facebook through a personal page. 

Therefore, the first step is to create a Fan Page that allows you to attract users who have your same interests.


Number of followers:


 the amount can vary depending on the monetization source you want to use.


 In some cases, having less than 10,000 followers makes the task of generating income more complicated.


 
Real and segmented followers:


 it will be useless to have thousands of followers if they are fake. That is why you must attract real users who are interested in what you offer.


 In addition, it is essential that you segment them according to aspects such as the country of origin, their preferences or the hobbies that define them.


Frequent activity:


 you have to be constantly active on your Facebook page, make publications with the appropriate frequency.


 Otherwise, it will be almost impossible for you to make money on this platform.


A high reach:


 this is what will determine the performance of your page. If it's being low, you should analyze your content strategy. 

You should try to make the range as high as possible.


2.- Sell your products or services


When it comes to making money, there is no better way to do it than by selling your own products or services.


 And the same happens on Facebook pages.

 Remember that those who follow you do so because they are interested in what you offer them.


You can offer digital products such as ebooks you have written, guides, manuals, downloadable articles, or online courses created by your brand.


 It is important that you focus on making original content that attracts users in order to retain them.


To be able to sell these contents, you not only have to upload them to a storage platform and publish it on the social network.


 You will also need to have a strategy to help you move it properly.


3.- Advertise affiliate products


To opt for affiliate marketing to make money with Facebook, you just have to join other companies' programs. 


They provide you with their catalog so that you can mention their products.


 What is the profit? In most cases, a commission for each sale made.


 Although some also pay to derive traffic to their websites.


This is a good alternative if you don't have your own products or services.

 Companies like Clickbank, Amazon, have this type of program. 


You just have to select the one that best suits your page and that offers the products that interest your followers.


Do not forget to publish your affiliate link, since it is through it the way in which you will get your commission. 


Also, put a reference image and a short description.


4.- Publish ads with Facebook Ads

 

If you have the ability to pay to get traffic, then do so because you can convert it into income.


 Facebook offers you the Audience Network platform with which you can achieve this.


 It is an efficient way to promote both your own and affiliate products and receive money for them.


Keep in mind that to be truly profitable, the investment you make in advertising must be less than the profits you can get from sales.


7.- Sell your own FanPage

 

Today, there are people who are dedicated to creating Facebook pages with different themes. 


They begin to publish related content that may interest a specific audience to gain followers.


 And once they reach the desired number of fans, they sell them to other brands.


In this way, these companies do not start from scratch on the platform.


 If you want to monetize in this way, you will have to be patient to get the desired reach. 


But if you succeed you can make this a trade that will generate good sums of money.


In fact, these pages are usually sold in ranges that can go from 150 to 2,500 dollars.


 It all depends on the number of fans and the reach of the page.


 You just have to carefully analyze which one best suits your goals. Once you make the decision you can start generating income.
