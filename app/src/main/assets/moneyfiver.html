 What is Fiverr and how it works?


Fiverr is a platform created to offer and sell digital products or services over the Internet.


 It works as a site where you can post your skills and earn money.


To begin with Fiverr is a simple and easy to explore website, which you can enter to buy or sell digital products or services.


 this means that they are only products that can be sent by a computer such as audios, writings, and translations, nothing physical.

 

Photo front.

 

The Fiverr website was created in 2009 and already has more than 25 million purchases and sales.


 that is, if you want to earn extra money or earn a living as a freelancer, Fiverr is the place you have to be.


It works like an online marketplace, where people sign up and post their offer and buyers choose the post that offers just what they need.


 It's that easy, without interviews or hiring.


For buyers, it is as simple as entering the site, choosing a category, selecting an offer, receiving the service, and making the payment.


For sellers the process is just as practical, you just have to register and choose what you want to sell.


 which does not have to be anything complex or out of this world.

It can be a translation into another language, write a text of 500 words, give a dance lesson, give advice, sing a poem, design a logo.


 Whatever you know how to do, something small, quick, and easy.


When you choose what you want to sell, you will make a publication or gig in which you offer your service or your digital product.


 remember that these are microservices, so the prices should be between $ 5 and $ 10 on average, for someone that is just starting on the platform.

How to sign up for  Fiverr


1. Go to the Fiverr website


The first step to register on Fiverr is to enter their website to fill out the registration form.


 Creating your account on Fiverr is free and it won't take you more than 2 minutes to do so.

 

2 . Sign up with your email


When you're on the website Fiverr, you must go to the top right and to click the button that says Join ( Join ) to start the registration process.

 

How to post a gig on Fiverr


Posting a gig on Fiverr is very simple: log into your Fiverr account with your email and password, and then click on Become a seller (become a seller).


 From there, you can create your ad and publish it to start making money with Fiverr.


The gigs are the way they are called the publications in Fiverr.


they are nothing more than small publications with images in which you offer your product, service, or skill.


When you have your account, I recommend that you take a walk through the categories, so you can see all the options you have to sell.


The next step is to log into your account and make your idea come true. At the top of the screen, you will find a button that says "Become a seller" ( Become a seller ).


In the first form, you are going to put information about yourself, your profession, and your tastes.


The first thing that you are going to fill in the general view of your gig ( Overview ), then you are going to place the title of your gig in ( Gig title ).


 it should be a direct and simple answer to the question "What will you do?" for example "I will translate 500 words for $ 5".


Then select from a category that I explained above, and some keywords from your publication.


The next step is one of the ones that interest us the most, how much are you going to charge? ; 


To earn money on Fiverr you can create a basic package or 3 different types of packages with special services depending on what you are going to sell.


To translate each of the items on the list you can copy and paste them into a Google translator or use the web page translation tool.


On the next page, you will make a description of what exactly you offer.


 for example, if you make greeting cards you can indicate the size, styles, format, and delivery time.


In the ( requirements ) module, you will write the requirements and instructions that the buyer must follow to successfully obtain your product or service.


 such as filling out a form, sending a file, or choosing a model.


You're almost done, you just have to add images, documents, or videos to your gig gallery and publish.


And out! Click "Done" ( Done ) and get ready to start making money on Fiverr because buyers can see your post.

Tips for selling more on Fiverr


The Fiverr page has a huge number of sellers, therefore, it is not enough to publish your gig and that's it.


 it is necessary to make up your publication, make it more attractive to buyers to stand out, and thus be able to sell more on Fiverr.


To improve your gig and make it more attractive, I will give you some very useful and practical tips.


An advantage of Fiverr is that it allows you to customize your gig as you wish.


so it is very important to take into account certain attributes, such as delivery time, number of sales, competition in the market, reputation, etc.


The main key to being successful on Fiverr is to have all these attributes working for you.


Remember that generally, buyers on Fiverr looking for immediate solutions, tasks that you can deliver within a period of no more than 48 hours.


By having a short delivery time, your gig can stand out from other gigs in the same category, that is, from the competition.


When pricing your gig, it's also important to consider a balance between quantity and quality.


Quality should always be the highest possible because your reputation will depend on it.


As for the quantity, it is a personal decision, because many people prefer to do many things of good quality, but short and cheap.


To mention an example, translating 500 words, on the other hand, other people seek to do fewer things but at a higher cost, such as translating a book.


As a beginner, it is better to do a lot of small things and of excellent quality at a competent price.


 whatever you think is convenient, so you can gradually increase your reputation; the minimum price for Fiverr is $ 5.


There is a lot of competition in the Fiverr market.


 it is recommended that you dedicate yourself to observe gigs related to your service and see what things they do not offer that you can offer.


or in the best of cases improve your gig, either by reducing the time delivery, price, etc.


Regarding reputation, it is an attribute that depends more on quality than on the number of sales, strive to always give the best of yourself.


To earn money on Fiverr you don't need advanced knowledge, you just have to choose to provide a service that you like to do.


In addition, Fiverr has a very complete section to help you complete the profile, publish a gig, and many other tools that will help you grow very quickly.
